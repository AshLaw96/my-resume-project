    Python North West Meetups

    Python North West meet monthly on the third Thursday of each month to discuss all things Python. Open to Pythonistas of all ages and abilities for talks, discussion and programming sessions, come along if you want to learn, teach, or just talk about anything and everything Python related.     Creative Coding Sheffield

    This is a Sheffield-based group for everyone interested in the creativity of code. We are not just programmers, we're also artists, educators, practitioners and hobbyists working with or near code for creative reasons. Creative coding comes in many shapes and forms such as using code to make music, generate visual arts, design new wearable tech-fashion, explore literature, or to engage the next generation of programmers within the classroom. Whether you're a seasoned creative coder, have just starting your coding adventure or are simply curious about creative coding we welcome you with open arms. Creative Coding Sheffield
